Mashed Potatoes

Mashed potatoes are a family favorite and it was time I shared the recipe. There are so many versions of mashed potato recipes on the web. The recipe that I make is a homely, easy and one bowl recipe.

Ingredients

  • Cottage cheese
  • Vegetable soup
  • Creamed corn
  • Boiling potatoes
  • Grilled vegetables

Let's Cook

  • Rinse 500 grams of potatoes in water first using a colander. Brush off any mud if any. Rinse really well. Drain the water and then peel the potatoes.
  • Chop them in 1.5 to 2 inches cubes.
  • While the potatoes are cooking check at intervals. Meanwhile, you can chop garlic and the fresh herbs if using them.
  • Add ½ teaspoon finely chopped or minced garlic. Please note that you can add garlic even while boiling potatoes which at times I even do.
  • On a low to medium-low flame begin to heat the pan. Stir occasionally. The butter will begin to melt initially.
  • Add 2 to 3 teaspoons of fresh mixed herbs or ½ to 1 teaspoon of dried mixed herbs. I have used a mix of fresh rosemary and parsley here.
