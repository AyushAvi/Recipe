Mac and Cheese

This Mac and Cheese Recipe is creamy, cheesy and delicious. The ultimate in comfort food, this pasta dish is made with a gooey homemade sauce of butter, milk and melted cheese. A light topping of breadcrumbs adds a fabulous crunch.

Ingredients

  • Mac
  • Cheese
  • Salt and Sugar
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Light Cream

Let's Cook

  • Cook the pasta as per the instructions on the package. I first heated 4 cups water, plus ½ teaspoon salt to a vigorous boil and then added the pasta.
  • Drain the pasta in a colander or strainer.
  • Add diced butter to the flour mixture and cut it into the flour until the largest pieces are pea-sized. It’s easy with a pastry blender.
  • Now add ⅓ cup breadcrumbs to the melted butter. If you do not have store-bought breadcrumbs, you can easily make your own. Simply take 3 to 4 slices of bread that are 2 to 3 days old, tear them apart and grind them in a dry grinder or mixer.
  • Keep the flame to the lowest and pour the milk in a gentle stream. Pour the milk with one hand and stir the sauce mixture with the other. The milk has to be chilled, so that lumps do not form. If you use hot, warm or room temperature milk, then lumps will form.
  • Bake. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet and bake at 450˚F for 12-15 minutes or until golden. Brush hot biscuits with melted butter.
